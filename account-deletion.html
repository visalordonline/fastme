Delete Your FastMe Account

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. If you wish to delete your FastMe account, please follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the FastMe app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Select Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your request.

If you cannot access your account, send a deletion request to:

support@fastme.online

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your profile will be permanently removed.
  • Your personal information will be deleted.
  • Your bookings and account history may be removed.
  • Some information may be retained where required by law or for fraud prevention.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are normally processed within 30 days.
