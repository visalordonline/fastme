Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Child Safety

FastMe is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for everyone using our platform. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Zero Tolerance Policy

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) is strictly prohibited.
  • Any form of child exploitation, grooming, or abuse is prohibited.
  • Users engaging in illegal activities will be permanently banned.
  • We cooperate with law enforcement agencies whenever legally required.

Reporting Abuse

Users can report inappropriate messages, profiles, or behaviour directly through the FastMe application.

Every report is reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken.

Our Safety Measures

🛡 Account Moderation

Accounts violating our policies are suspended or permanently removed.

🚨 Abuse Reporting

Users can easily report harmful content or suspicious behaviour.

⚖ Compliance

We comply with applicable child safety and online protection laws.

🔒 Privacy

User reports are handled confidentially and securely.
