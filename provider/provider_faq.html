play_circle_outline Getting Started

What is Fastme?

expand_more

Fastme is a social connection platform designed to help people meet, connect, and build meaningful relationships. Whether you're looking for new friends, dates, or professional connections, Fastme provides a safe and fun environment to do so.

How do I create an account?

expand_more

Creating an account is easy:

  • Download the Fastme app or visit our website
  • Click "Sign Up" and choose your registration method (email, Google, or Facebook)
  • Enter your details (name, email, date of birth, etc.)
  • Verify your email address
  • Complete your profile with a photo and bio
  • Start connecting with others!

Is Fastme free to use?

expand_more

Yes! Fastme is free to download and use for basic features. We offer premium features and memberships for users who want additional benefits like unlimited messaging, advanced filters, and ad-free browsing.

What age do I need to be to use Fastme?

expand_more

You must be at least 18 years old to use Fastme. We verify ages during registration to ensure a safe community for adults.

Can I use Fastme on multiple devices?

expand_more

Yes! You can access your Fastme account on multiple devices using the same email/login credentials. Your profile, messages, and connections will sync across all devices.

account_box_outline Account Management

How do I edit my profile?

expand_more

To edit your profile:

  • Go to your Profile page
  • Click the "Edit Profile" button
  • Update your information (name, bio, photos, etc.)
  • Click "Save Changes" to apply updates

How do I change my password?

expand_more

To change your password:

  • Go to Account Settings
  • Select "Security"
  • Click "Change Password"
  • Enter your current password and new password
  • Click "Update" to save

How do I delete my account?

expand_more

To permanently delete your account:

  • Go to Account Settings
  • Select "Privacy"
  • Click "Delete Account"
  • Follow the confirmation steps
  • Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Note: This action is irreversible.

Can I change my email address?

expand_more

Yes, you can change your email address in your Account Settings under "Contact Information". You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

How do I recover a forgotten password?

expand_more

If you forget your password:

  • Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
  • Enter your email address
  • Check your email for a reset link
  • Click the link and create a new password
chat_bubble_outline Messaging & Stories

How do I send a message?

expand_more

To send a message:

  • Go to Messages and select a chat
  • Type your message in the text box
  • Click "Send" or press Enter
  • Your message will appear instantly

Can I delete a message after sending?

expand_more

Yes! You can delete messages you've sent within a certain timeframe. Long-press on the message and select "Delete". Deleted messages will show as "[This message was deleted]" to both parties.

What are Stories?

expand_more

Stories are temporary posts that disappear after 24 hours (or your chosen duration). They're a fun way to share moments from your day. Stories can include photos, videos, captions, and more. Only your connections can see your stories.

How do I post a story?

expand_more

To post a story:

  • Click the "+" button in the bottom-right corner
  • Add your caption and/or photo
  • Select how long the story should last (24 hours, 3 days, 7 days)
  • Click "Share Story" and it will be visible to your connections

Can I control who sees my messages?

expand_more

Yes! In your Privacy Settings, you can control who can message you:

  • Everyone
  • Only connections
  • Only mutual likes
  • Nobody (disable messages)

How do I report a user?

expand_more

To report a user:

  • Open their profile
  • Click the three-dot menu (...)
  • Select "Report User"
  • Choose the reason for reporting
  • Provide additional details if needed
  • Click "Submit Report"

Our team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action.

How do I block someone?

expand_more

To block a user:

  • Open their profile
  • Click the three-dot menu (...)
  • Select "Block User"
  • Confirm your action

Blocked users cannot see your profile, message you, or interact with your content.

What should I do if I encounter inappropriate content?

expand_more

If you encounter inappropriate content:

  • Report the content immediately
  • Block the user
  • Do not engage or respond to the content
  • Contact our support team if needed

Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes swift action against violations.

Is Fastme verified secure? (SSL/HTTPS)

expand_more

Yes! Fastme uses SSL/HTTPS encryption for all data transmissions. You can verify this by looking for the padlock icon in your browser's address bar. All sensitive information is encrypted and securely stored.

build Technical Issues

The app is crashing. What should I do?

expand_more

If the app keeps crashing:

  • Force close the app completely
  • Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > Fastme > Clear Cache)
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest app version from the App Store/Google Play
  • If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app

I'm having connection issues. How do I fix this?

expand_more

To fix connection issues:

  • Check your internet connection (WiFi or mobile data)
  • Turn WiFi off and on again
  • Restart your device
  • Try a different network (switch between WiFi and mobile data)
  • Disable any VPN services temporarily
  • Clear app cache and reinstall if needed

Why isn't my location updating?

expand_more

If your location isn't updating:

  • Check that location permissions are enabled for Fastme in your phone settings
  • Ensure your GPS/Location is turned on
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Try disabling location and re-enabling it
  • Go to your profile and manually update your location

What devices is Fastme available on?

expand_more

Fastme is available on:

  • iOS: iPhone, iPad (App Store)
  • Android: Android phones and tablets (Google Play)
  • Web: Desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

How do I enable/disable notifications?

To manage notifications:

How do I cancel my subscription?

expand_more

To cancel your subscription:
